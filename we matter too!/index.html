Welcome to We Matter Too! Foundation

We are dedicated to improving the lives of elderly individuals. Our mission is to provide support, care, and resources to enhance their quality of life.

About Us

The We Matter Too! Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to raising donations from around the world for the care of seniors and dedicated to sanctioning people who commit acts of violence or neglect against seniors. Our goals include ending:

Chronic diseases: such as osteoarthritis, diabetes, hypertension and high cholesterol, which can affect their quality of life and autonomy.

Neurological diseases: such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's, which can cause loss of memory, motor skills and cognitive abilities.

Myocardial infarction: considered one of the main causes of death in older people, it is important to detect early symptoms, such as pain in the chest, arm or abdomen.

Social isolation and loneliness: can seriously affect the mental health of older people, which can lead to depression, anxiety and other disorders.

Injuries: such as blows to the head, which can be common in older people and require immediate medical attention.

Cognitive changes: such as decreased memory and ability to concentrate, which can affect your ability to make decisions and perform everyday tasks.
